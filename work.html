Haptic Cube 19.03 - 19.04 / individual project

Visually impaired people are provided with fewer opportunities for experience than non-visual impaired people. In particular, in the field of visual exhibitions and art works, many works are prohibited from being touched, so visually impaired people are limited in enjoying art. Haptic cube has meaning as an object enjoyed by the sense of touch. It provides an experience of traveling the world with a finger tip by engraving a pattern on each side of the two cubes.

Blink 20.03 - 20.06 / individual project

Blink is a mask patch that detects and expresses the speaker's speech. Masks have become New Normal's daily necessities, which block not only the coronavirus, but also non-verbal communication through the face. Blink provides a refreshing conversation experience by naturally recognizing each other's speech in a face-to-face communication situation.
